# Production Accounting Plan

## Overview
Dedicated plan for production accounting, handling revenue, costs, and reporting.

## Processes
- Volume reconciliation.
- Royalty calculations.
- Cost allocation.
- Financial reporting.

## International Variations
- Tax regimes (e.g., severance taxes, VAT).
- Accounting standards (GAAP, IFRS).
- Currency and exchange rates.

## Calculations
- Allocation algorithms (e.g., proration).
- Economic models for profitability.

## UI/GFX
- Accounting dashboards.
- Audit trails visualizations.

## Integration
- Ties into production data from other phases.
- Separate module for compliance and accuracy.
