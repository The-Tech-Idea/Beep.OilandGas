# Production Phase Plan

## Overview
Production phase focuses on extracting oil, maintaining operations, and optimizing output.

## Processes
- Well monitoring and maintenance.
- Production forecasting.
- Enhanced recovery techniques.
- Safety and environmental compliance.

## International Variations
- In UK North Sea, mature field management.
- In Venezuela, nationalized operations.
- Tax regimes affect profitability (e.g., production sharing contracts).

## Calculations
- Decline curve analysis (DCA).
- Nodal analysis for flow optimization.
- Economic calculations for royalties and taxes.

## UI/GFX
- Real-time dashboards for production data.
- Heat maps for field performance.
- Wellbore diagrams.

## Integration with Lifecycle
- Ongoing phase with feedback to accounting.
