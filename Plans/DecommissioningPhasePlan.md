# Decommissioning Phase Plan

## Overview
Decommissioning involves safely abandoning the field and restoring the environment.

## Processes
- Well plugging and abandonment.
- Facility dismantling.
- Site remediation.
- Regulatory reporting and closure.

## International Variations
- OSPAR Convention in North Sea for offshore decommissioning.
- In US, BOEMRE oversees Gulf of Mexico.
- Cost allocation differs (e.g., operator vs. government).

## Calculations
- Decommissioning cost estimates.
- Environmental impact assessments.
- Asset valuation for sale or disposal.

## UI/GFX
- Progress tracking visualizations.
- Environmental monitoring maps.
- 3D models of removal plans.

## Integration with Lifecycle
- Final phase, closing the cycle.
